# Smaller improvements (2022)

- **Poll common form.** The poll creation form was rebuilt with a shared design across all poll types, making the interface more consistent when switching between simple polls, proposals, dot votes, and ranked choices.
- **Show winner in outcome.** When sharing a poll outcome, the winning option is now highlighted and listed first in the outcome statement.
- **"This is Loomio" banner.** Visitors viewing content without an account now see a banner explaining what Loomio is and offering a trial, helping new people understand the platform.
- **Paginated poll votes.** The poll results page now loads votes in pages rather than all at once, making it much faster on polls with hundreds of voters.
- **Brand update.** The Loomio logo and brand colors were updated across the app.
- **Remove self from poll.** You can now remove yourself from a poll's voter list if you were added but don't want to participate.
