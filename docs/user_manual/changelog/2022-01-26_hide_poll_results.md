# Hide poll results

Polls now have more granular control over when results are visible.

## Settings

When creating or editing a poll, choose from these result visibility options:

- **Show results immediately.** Results are visible to everyone who can see the poll while voting is open.
- **Hide results until the poll closes.** No one sees the result breakdown until voting ends.
- **Show results after you vote.** You can see results only after casting your own vote.

The setting can be changed after the poll is created from the poll menu.
