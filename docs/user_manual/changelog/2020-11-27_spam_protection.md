# Spam and abuse protection

Several improvements help keep your instance free of spam accounts.

- **Spam user denial.** Accounts flagged as spam are automatically prevented from creating content or sending invitations.
- **reCAPTCHA support.** Instance admins can enable reCAPTCHA on the sign-up form to block automated account creation.
- **Content locale detection.** The system now detects the language of user-generated content, helping moderators identify accounts that may not be legitimate.
