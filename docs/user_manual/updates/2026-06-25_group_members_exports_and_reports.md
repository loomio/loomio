# Group members, exports, and reports

Several group administration and reporting workflows were improved.

## Search member titles

The group members panel can now search member titles as well as names and other member fields.

This helps admins find people by role, position, or responsibility when titles are stored on memberships.

## Poll CSV exports include member details

Poll CSV exports now include member details.

This gives admins and coordinators more useful context when reviewing poll participation outside Loomio.

## Participation report performance

The participation report was optimized so larger reports can load more reliably.

This is most noticeable for larger organizations, active groups, or reports spanning many threads and polls.

## Bookmark visibility on hidden poll results

The Save bookmark action is hidden on votes when poll results are not yet visible.

This avoids exposing controls around vote details before the poll settings allow results to be seen.
