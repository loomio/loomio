# Scheduled poll opening times

Polls can now be scheduled to open for voting at a future date and time rather than immediately.

## How it works

Until the scheduled time, the poll is visible to the group but not votable, and shows an "Opening in..." countdown. Voting-open notifications are sent when the poll actually opens, not when it's created, so people aren't notified before there's anything to do.

## Poll form changes

The poll creation form was reworked with a clearer voting-duration section that shows opening and closing dates together, a "More settings" accordion to reduce clutter for the common case, and a redesigned date picker.
