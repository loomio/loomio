# Explore page improvements

The public groups directory (Explore) is easier to browse and easier for admins to control.

## Sorting and listing

Explore now offers four sort orders for the groups directory: most members, fewest members, newest, and oldest. Group admins get a clearer "List in the groups directory?" choice in group settings, with better-worded descriptions of what listing means for privacy. "Anyone can join" is available as a group option even when public account creation is closed at the instance level.

## Restricting Explore to signed-in users

Instance admins can now require sign-in to view the Explore page. When enabled, visitors who aren't signed in are sent to the login page instead of seeing the public groups directory.
