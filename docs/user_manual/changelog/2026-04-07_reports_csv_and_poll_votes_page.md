# Report CSV export and a dedicated poll votes page

Each table in the group or organization Reports page now has a "Download CSV" button, letting admins export report data for use in spreadsheets without extra tooling.

Polls also gained their own "votes" page listing every participant's response, with filtering by option and searching by voter name. It's linked from the participation count on the poll's results chart, making it much easier to review who voted for what on polls with many participants.
